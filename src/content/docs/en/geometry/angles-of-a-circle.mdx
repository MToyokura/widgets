## Inscribed Angles

An inscribed angle is an angle formed by three points on the circumference of a circle. The inscribed angle subtended by the same arc is always the same.

<D3InscribedAngle />

## Central Angles

A central angle is an angle whose vertex is the center of a circle. The central angle is twice the size of the inscribed angle subtended by the same arc.

<D3CentralAngle />

## Thales' Theorem

Thales' theorem states that if A, B, and C are distinct points on a circle where the line AC is a diameter, then the angle ∠ABC is a right angle.
