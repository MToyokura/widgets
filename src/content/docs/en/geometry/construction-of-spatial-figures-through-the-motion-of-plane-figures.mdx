## Creating a Plane by Translating a Line Segment

When a line segment is moved in parallel, the trace of its movement forms a single plane.

## Creating a Curved Surface by Rotating a Line

The sides of a cylinder or a cone can be considered as a curved surface created by a single line segment rotating around a fixed line (the axis).
